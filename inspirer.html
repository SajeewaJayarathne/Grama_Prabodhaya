Inspirer

Inspirer was an English language usage and communication skills enhancement program focused on grade 9 to 11 students in Ranugalla area, which was successfully held on 22nd and 23rd April, 2017.
This program consisted of 2 phases where the 1st phase was a two-day workshop where students of the schools around the village participated and next was the establishment of student communities in each school where students are provided with manuals which would cover up a specific period and will be implemented on a weekly basis as a part of the normal student curriculum.
During the two-day programme, the participants were given opportunities to engage in activities that improve their self - confidence through providing a platform for the participants to sharpen and exhibit their language proficiency.
The introduction of the student activity manual would ensure that the students are continuously engaged in uplifting their communicative skills in English.
