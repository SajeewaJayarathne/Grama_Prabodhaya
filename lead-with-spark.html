Lead with Spark

As students who live in a rural area, they lack the facility of interacting with many people of different cultures, and that might lead those students to hide and go backward when they step into the society in their future. Therefore, as a continuation of phase 1 of the project Grama Prabodhaya, Lead with Spark, a one-day leadership development programme was held on the 8th of April, 2017 for the students of Ranugalla Kanishta Vidyalaya. The camp was conducted in collaboration with the Prefects’ Guild of Ananda College, Colombo. This was mainly targeted the prefects of the school to develop their leadership skills and to streamline the procedures of their Prefects’ Body.
