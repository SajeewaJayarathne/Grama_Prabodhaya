Healing Hands

Healing Hands was a Medical Camp organized as another subproject under the project Grama Prabodhaya. It was a full-day medical camp which successfully completed on 11th December, 2016 by treating patients in the area with the gentle hands of many doctors, nurses, PHI and MOH officers, local health workers and medical lab technicians who tended to the task readily and with open arms, and two sessions that were held to educate the villagers about non-communicable diseases and nutrition. The medical camp comprised of a general checkups and consultation facility, a dental facility, a random and fasting blood sugar check-up facility, and a pharmacy. The idea behind this project was to make quality and reliable health care available and accessible for the much needy people in rural areas which at times is a luxury they cannot afford.
