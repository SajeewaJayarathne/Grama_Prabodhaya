Bridge of Hearts

Bridge of Hearts was initiated to donate books for the library to be opened at Ranugalla Kanishta Vidyalaya since they do not have the luxury of reading books as they desire. A few book collection were held within the university premises, and at the Bandaranaike Memorial International Conference Hall for six days during the International Book Fair - 2016. More than 1500 books were collected and they are successfully sorted into several categories; such as Educational books, Novels, English and Sinhala Storybooks, Past Papers up to Ordinary Level, etc. Further, it is proposed to donate packs of stationery items along with the aforementioned sets of books.
