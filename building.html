Science Laboratory and Library

As the main project under Grama Prabodaya Phase 1, we have started building a fully equipped science laboratory and a library for the school, along with the London Stock Exchange Group, who is the main sponsor of this great endeavor. The building is currently undergoing construction successfully along with the timeline, which proceeded after laying the foundation stone on the 11th of December 2016, by the hands of the Principal, Mrs. Renuka Fernando, the President of RotaractMora, Rtr. Chamal Kuruppu and the District Representative Rtr. Ridwan Shariffdeen.
As the school has no proper place for the science lab, we wished to construct a room complete with all the facilities necessary to conduct the science practical sessions under the subject curriculum of students from grade 6 to 11. As the practical experiments of science should go hand in hand with the theoretical knowledge to gain the solid foundation of the subject, we believe that the students will greatly benefit from this. The proper practical knowledge in science will also be of great importance for the students who wished to pursue their further education (Advanced Level) in the streams of Bio-Science and Physical Science.
Along with the science laboratory, two similar rooms are being constructed to be used as the school library, and as a classroom. The library will facilitate further research and will inculcate the habit of reading within the student community.
